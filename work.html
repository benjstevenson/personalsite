Warrior Philosophy

I love martials arts, especially Muay Thai and BJJ (as they're the most practical). But more than that, I love the philosophy of great warriors, of strategy, of combat. I think it teaches you a lot about your mind, courage, calmness under pressure, and business. I made a channel breaking down the lessons and takeaways from greatest warriors throughout history. I grew it to >40,000 subscribers + 2.4 million views in about 5 months. Would've kept it going but there's not much advertising money in the martial arts space + the UFC kept copyrighting my videos (totally fair, but still annoying), and I needed money.
